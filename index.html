> interrupt-router

Ping Island stays quiet until an approval, prompt, review, or failure actually needs an operator.

> notch-actions

Approve tools, deny requests, and answer follow-ups from a native control surface instead of chasing tabs.

> focus-return

Jump back to iTerm2, Ghostty, Terminal.app, tmux panes, and IDE windows that originated the run.

> codex-sync

Tracks Codex hooks, app-server threads, and fallback parsing so active sessions stay coherent.

> soundpacks

Import local sound packs or remap event signals so the app sounds like your own workflow.

> mascots

Assign per-client mascot overrides across the notch, session list, and hover preview surfaces.
